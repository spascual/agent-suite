### Fast Product Iteration

- Ideation: Identify underserved painpoints/problems in your daily life. LIVE AN INTERESTING LIFE.
- Ship first, ship fast: Get started, lower your bar. Even a new crafted workflow or design can attract customers.
- Checklist before launch: Waitlist page, feedback collection workflow, analytics integration (PostHog), and build a community in Reddit by participating in your underserved niche.
